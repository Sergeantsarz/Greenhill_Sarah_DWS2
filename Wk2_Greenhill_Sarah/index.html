We all deserve the same rights and opportunities.

We as human beings seek to put everything and everyone into a box and categorize him/her/it. Regardless of the box we fit into at any given moment, we ALL deserve the same basic human rights and privileges. We all deserve to be seen as equals.

L G B T Q?

"LGBTQ" is an acronym that originated in the 1990s and replaced what was formerly known as "the gay community." The acronymn was created to be more inclusive of diverse groups. LGBTQ stands for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer (and/or questioning) individuals/identities.

Education

Education about the issues surrounding the human rights campaign, gay marriage, gender identity, and sexuality is very, extremely important. People tend to misunderstand or develop incorrect conclusions because they are ignorant to the facts and information that could show them otherwise.

Activism

Standing alone, one cannot get much done and there is a relatively narrow chance that your voice may be heard. Together is how people can affect positive change in the world and the more people we can get to stick with one another against negativity and ignorance surrounding LGBTQ issues, the better.
